I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), working under the supervision of Prof. Linghe Kong and Prof. Guihai Chen. 
I received my Ph.D. from SJTU and my B.E. from the School of Computer Engineering and Science, Shanghai University.

My research focuses on Network for AI and Machine Learning Systems, with specific interests spanning high-performance AI computing networks (MLSys, AI Infrastructure, RDMA Transport), intelligent network operations (AI for Network), programmable networks (SmartNIC, Programmable Switch), and satellite intelligence and communication.

I have published 7 papers in CCF-A ranked journals and conferences (6 as first author, 1 as co-first author), along with 10+ collaborative papers. My work has been recognized with 3 Best Paper / Best Student Paper Awards at international conferences. I have been honored with the Shanghai Super Postdoctoral Incentive Program, the Shanghai Computer Society Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award, and the SJTU Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award.

I currently lead a NSFC Young Scientists Fund project on "Cross-Domain Multi-Datacenter Converged AI Computing Networks" and serve as a sub-project lead for an NSFC Key Enterprise Joint Fund project on "Key Technologies for Traffic Measurement in Programmable Networks." My research is conducted in close collaboration with Alibaba Cloud, Huawei, and China Telecom Cloud, with several outcomes already deployed at industrial scale.
